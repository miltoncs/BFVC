Who We Are

Mission

To proclaim and exalt Jesus as a community of disciples who make disciples.

Vision

We seek to be a people who value: Proclamation, Worship, Discipleship, Community, Serving. All in obedience to The Lord.

If the greatest gift of our lives is to know Christ, certainly our greatest endeavours would be the ones spent exalting and proclaiming Him. And if that would be our greatest endeavours, making disciples would be among our finest and most meaningful movements.
